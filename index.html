Riyadh‘ Winter Wonderland

Get ready to fully immerse yourself in all things festive in Riyadh Winter Wonderland, where array of attractions, activities and entertainment for visitors of all ages are waiting for you. With spectacular rides and attractions, including the largest ice rink in the middle east, street food Riyadh‘s Winter Wonderland will be one of the main festival‘s venue of Riyadhseason.

The Night Garden

During Riyadh season, a world of enchantment comes to Al Murabba Gardens at king Abdulaziz Historical Center. The place has been turned into magical NightGarden, where the Tree of Wisdom talks and fairies fly before your eyes. Take this unbelievable journey with the whole family and watch magic unfold. Prepare your best camera because you will take a lot of photos there.

Nabd AL Riyadh

Another main festival zone is “Nabd Al Riyadh“, which literally means “Riyadh Pulse”. This zone will focus more on Saudi traditions and folklorics from all over the kingdom.There will be 3D light show, shows inspired by saudi culture, folkloric dances, exhibitions, food and exclusive shops for Riyadh Season..

MASMAKMUSEUM

HISTORICAL-MASMAK

IN THE EARLY HOURS OF January 14, 1902, the garrison inside the Masmak Fortress slept as the forces of Abdulaziz bin Abdul Rahman bin Faisal Al Saud gathered and prepared for attack. Taking the fortress by surprise, Abdulaziz captured the fort and took control of Riyadh, his ancestral home after having lived in exile in Kuwait. From this fortress, Abdulaziz Al Saud conquered and united the different kingdoms and provinces that make up the modern Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. RAED MORE
